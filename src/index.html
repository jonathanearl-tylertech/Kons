Founded in Lebanon in 1967, Kons Construction Company (KCC) has grown to become one of the Middle East's leading construction contractors.

We specialize in complex and prestigious construction and infrastructure projects. Our portfolio includes some of the regiion's most iconic landmarks, from super high-rise luxury developments, to five start hotels, hospitals and intricately sophisticated smart buildings.

We have exacting HSE and sustanability standards and the health and safty of both our employees and the general public is given utmost priority.
